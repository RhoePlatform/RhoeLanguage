## Design Commitments
- The canonical AST is the ontological form of the language.
- `Artifact` is the universal root.
- `RhoeJSON` is the canonical serialized notation.
- Surface syntax is authoritative only through valid lowering into the canonical AST.
- Target emissions are never semantic authorities.
